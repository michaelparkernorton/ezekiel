Why are there two donation options?

• On GofundMe's website, when donating you are asked to tip, and along with that 3% of your donation goes to a transaction fee.

• With Venmo, you donate to Michael (one of the creators), and 100% of the money goes to Ezekiel. No transaction fees, and no tip needed!
